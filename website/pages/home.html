    • GNTL Information:

  • GNTL is a decentralized public blockchain project and is fully open source. The development team consists of enthusiasts who have been dealing with cryptography and computer science for a long time, and have now decided to give this coin a go.
  • User friendly and community driven.
  • Anonymous and untraceable.
  • Open source and open minded.
  • Fast and secure.
  • GNTL is based on the ArQmA codebase, which is based on the Monero codebase and is constantly enhanced with new features, developed by the team itself or the ArQmA team.
  • The GNTL Network uses the RandomARQ PoW Hashing Algorithm, and is ASIC and Nicehash resistant to give everyone a chance to mine. The blockchain is fast to synchronize. Transfers are anonymous and untraceable using Bulletproof RingCT.
  • Open source and open minded.

    • Mining Details:

  • XMRig Algo: RandomARQ (RandomX variant for ArQmA)
  • Block Target Time: 2 Minutes
  • Blocks Per Day: 720 Blocks (on average)
  • Mineable with: CPU (favourable), GPU

  • Other Requirements: 2.5GB RAM per NUMA Node
